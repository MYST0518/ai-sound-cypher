## Customization

### YouTube Videos
Replace VIDEO_ID_1, VIDEO_ID_2, VIDEO_ID_3 in index.html with your actual YouTube video IDs

### Monthly Theme
Update the theme information in index.html or use the JavaScript function:
updateTheme('Theme Name', 'Description', 'Deadline')

### Social Links
Update Twitter and YouTube links in the footer section of index.html

### Google Form
Replace the Google Form link in the submission note section with your actual form URL
